PB & J Chicken Lettuce Wraps

Home

Description

I know, upon first hearing PB & J Chicken, one might think, "WHAT?! Peanut Butter and JELLY on chicken?!". Have no fear, the "J" in this PB & J stands for "Jalapeno". That's right, this chicken is smothered in my peanut butter and jalapeno sauce. This recipe was an impromptu substitution that became a household favorite.

My original recipe was for an asian style peanut butter sauce with green and red peppers, but in a mental lapse, I forgot that my husband doesn't like green or red peppers! A problem indeed! Well, I substituted those peppers with jalapenos, my husband LOVED it and I coined this new fave, the PB & J Chicken Lettuce Wraps

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground chicken
  • 2 Tbs peanut oil
  • 1/2 onion (finely diced)
  • 1/2 Tbs garlic (minced)
  • 1 cup jalapeno (diced)
  • 8 oz water chestnuts (finely diced)
  • Preferred lettuce for wraps

Sauce

  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 3 Tbs hoisin sauce
  • 1 Tbs sesame oil
  • 1 Tbs rice vinegar
  • 1 1/2 Tbs peanut butter
  • 1 Tbs honey
  • 2 tsp sweet/hot chili sauce
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 2 tsp powdered ginger

Steps

  1. Whisk together sauce ingredients until well combined. You may have to microwave for ~30 seconds to soften the peanut butter.
  2. Heat peanut oil in a frying pan and once hot, add the chicken, onion, and garlic.
  3. Cook until chicken browns and the onions are translucent; ~5-10 minutes.
  4. Add the jalapeno and water chestnuts and cook for another 5 minutes.
  5. Add the sauce and simmer on low heat until the chicken and veggies are evenly coated and the sauce thickens to your liking.
  6. Rinse lettuce leaves and prep to liking, serve alone or with rice or other toppings.
  7. ENJOY!
